Breakfast Burrito

Description:

Breakfast burritos are a nice, healthy, hardy way to start your day feeling full yet energized. Your choice of protein will help give that extra boost of energy as you work hard till lunch time.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Avacado
  • Cheese
  • Hot Sauce
  • Tortillas
  • Protein of your choice!

Steps:

  1. Add onions and garlic to pan on med/high heat.
  2. Create scrambled eggs in a pan with necessary seasonings.
  3. Prepare protein in whatever fashion is necessary.
  4. This is being added in the wrap of the burrito. Prepare as necessary.
  5. After all ingredients are prepared. Wrap all of them inside tortilla.
  6. Put burrito back on pan to make the tortilla crusty. Done!
