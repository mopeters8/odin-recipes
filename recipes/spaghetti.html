Spaghetti

Description:

Sweet and savory spaghetti to warm your belly up in the evening.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato Sauce of your choosing
  • Ground Sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook noodles on side.
  2. Brown sausage, with garlic and onions.
  3. Add sauce to browned sausage.
  4. After, mix noodles with the meaty sauce. Done!
